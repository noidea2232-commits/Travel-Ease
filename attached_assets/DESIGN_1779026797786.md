## Brand & Style

The design system is engineered for a premium, high-stakes logistics and travel environment. It evokes a sense of "First Class" reliability, combining the authority of deep naval tones with the prestige of metallic gold. The brand personality is sophisticated, exclusive, and precise.

The visual style follows a **Modern Corporate** aesthetic with a **Minimalist** finish. It utilizes high-contrast accents against an expansive dark canvas to create a focused, editorial experience. Expect heavy use of whitespace (or "darkspace"), sharp typography, and subtle metallic gradients that mimic the reflective quality of the provided logo.

## Colors

The palette is derived directly from the prestige of the logistics logo. The primary interaction color is **Gilded Gold**, used sparingly for high-value actions and branding. The secondary color is **Midnight Navy**, serving as the deep, immersive foundation of the interface.

- **Primary (Gilded Gold):** Used for primary buttons, active icons, and key branding elements.
- **Secondary (Midnight Navy):** The core background color.
- **Tertiary (Electric Blue):** Used for informational accents and secondary data visualizations to mirror the logo's motion trails.
- **Neutral (Slate):** Used for borders and de-emphasized text to maintain legibility without breaking the dark aesthetic.

## Typography

This design system uses a high-contrast typographic pairing to signal luxury. **Playfair Display** provides an authoritative, editorial feel for headings, while **Manrope** ensures technical precision and readability for functional UI elements.

All headers should utilize slightly tighter letter-spacing to appear "locked" and intentional. Body text should maintain generous line heights to prevent eye fatigue against the dark background.

## Layout & Spacing

The design system employs a **Fixed Grid** model for desktop to maintain an editorial, structured feel, transitioning to a **Fluid Grid** for mobile devices.

- **Desktop:** 12-column grid, 1280px max-width, 24px gutters.
- **Tablet:** 8-column grid, 24px margins.
- **Mobile:** 4-column grid, 16px margins.

Spacing follows a strict 8px linear scale. Large-scale sections should be separated by significant vertical padding (80px+) to emphasize the "Premium" feel and allow the gold accents to breathe.

## Elevation & Depth

Hierarchy is established through **Tonal Layering** and **Subtle Gold Outlines**. Rather than heavy shadows, which can feel muddy on deep navy backgrounds, we use surface lightening.

- **Level 0 (Base):** #050B1C (Midnight Navy).
- **Level 1 (Cards/Panels):** #0A142F (Slightly lighter navy).
- **Level 2 (Modals/Popovers):** #111D3D with a 1px solid border in 10% opacity Gold (#E3C06F1A).

To mimic the logo’s metallic sheen, "Gilded" elements may use a subtle linear gradient (45-degree angle) from #E3C06F to #B28C3F.

## Shapes

The shape language is "Soft" yet disciplined. While the luxury sector often leans toward sharp corners, we use a subtle radius to ensure the UI feels modern and accessible.

- **Standard Elements (Buttons, Inputs):** 4px (0.25rem).
- **Containers (Cards, Sections):** 8px (0.5rem).
- **Feature Elements:** 12px (0.75rem) for high-level dashboard widgets.

Avoid pill-shapes or fully rounded circles except for avatars or status indicators, as they conflict with the "Serious/Professional" brand tone.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid Gilded Gold background with Midnight Navy text. No border.
- **Secondary:** Transparent background with a 1.5px Gilded Gold border and Gold text.
- **Tertiary:** Text-only in Gold or White with a subtle underline on hover.

### Cards
Cards should not use shadows. Instead, use a subtle background color shift (Level 1 Elevation) and a very thin, low-opacity border. For "featured" content, a 2px top-border in Gold is recommended.

### Input Fields
Inputs use a dark navy fill (#0A142F) with a Slate border. On focus, the border transitions to Gilded Gold with a subtle outer glow of the same color (30% opacity).

### Lists & Navigation
Navigation items should use `label-caps` for a sense of hierarchy. The active state is indicated by a Gold vertical bar to the left of the item or a Gold underline.

### Chips/Tags
Small, low-profile badges with a dark navy fill and Gilded Gold text. Used primarily for status (e.g., "In Transit", "Delivered").
